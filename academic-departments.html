ACADEMIC DEPARTMENTS

An academic department is a division of a university or school faculty devoted to a particular academic discipline. In the United States, academic departments are typically responsible for offering courses, conducting research, and awarding degrees in their respective fields. They are also responsible for hiring and managing faculty members, and providing support for students.
